<p>點選資料夾date的PuTTy的puttygen.exe</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="256" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/3.png" width="500"></p>
<p><span><span>按下</span></span>Generate</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="490" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/4.png" width="500"></p>
<p>接著把它複製下來</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="493" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/5.png" width="500"></p>
<p>然後開啟自己的github的settings按下SSH and GPG keys</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="350" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/6.png" width="500"></p>
<p><span><span>新增</span></span>New SSH key</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="370" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/7.png" width="500"></p>
<p>把剛剛複製的貼上去後，按下Add SSH key</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="351" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/8.png" width="500"></p>
<p>以上設定好後按下Save private key</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="494" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/9.png" width="500"></p>
<p>打上檔案名稱 (要在後面打上&nbsp;<strong>.ppk</strong>)</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="282" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/10.png" width="500"></p>
<p>接著再開啟date的PuTTy的putty.exe&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><img caption="false" height="281" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/11.png" width="500"></strong></p>
<p>點選github.com並按下Load</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="490" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/12.png" width="500"></p>
<p>再去Proxy設定網路位置</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="488" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/13.png" width="500"></p>
<p>然後去Auth按Browse選取剛處存的檔案</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="500" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/14.png" width="500"></p>
<p>最後按下Save就完成了</p>
<p><img caption="false" height="488" src="https://a40923101.github.io/wcm2021/images/15.png" width="500"></p>
